About Us

Sarvesamanah Foundation is a dedicated non‑governmental organization working in the field of urban afforestation and environmental restoration since 2023. Based in Pune, Maharashtra, we focus on planting native tree species and ensuring their long‑term survival to build sustainable urban green ecosystems.

Our work goes beyond plantation — we take responsibility for nurturing, protecting, and monitoring trees so that they grow into resilient urban forests.

Our Mission

“To restore ecological balance by planting and nurturing native trees in urban and peri-urban regions.”

  • Offset tree loss due to urban construction and development
  • Revive and protect native biodiversity
  • Promote long-term environmental sustainability

Our Achievements

2,500+ Native Trees

Planted in 2023 with full‑year maintenance including watering and fire‑hazard safety.

Healthy Native Plants

2–3 year old plants, 5–6 ft tall, geo‑tagged, certified, and sourced from trusted nurseries.

100% Native Species

Jambhul, Karanj, Bhehda, Neem and other ecologically suitable native trees.

Taljai Hills Plantation (2024–25)

  • 2,400+ trees planted in Taljai Forest Zone (PMC area) in 2024
  • 4,000+ plantations in progress during 2025
  • Regular watering, grass cutting, and survival monitoring
  • Geo-tagged plantation with GPS-based photo documentation
  • Entire project implemented and funded by Sarvesamanah Foundation

Our In‑House Private Nursery

  • Dedicated private nursery for growing native species
  • Plants sourced from diverse eco‑regions
  • Focus on survival rate, strong root health, and field readiness
  • Supports large‑scale annual plantation targets

2025 Target

  • Plant and maintain 5,000+ native trees
  • Year-round care for previously planted saplings
  • Plantation at urban open spaces, PMC-approved lands, and public-private sites
  • Focus on biodiversity, longevity, and climate resilience
